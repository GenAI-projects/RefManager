RefManager Data Protection Agreement

By using RefManager, you agree to the following:

  1. You authorize RefManager to read/write only data required for citation management and library sync.
  2. You will not upload sensitive personal, clinical, or protected health data unless permitted by your institutional policy.
  3. You are responsible for verifying citation accuracy before publication.
  4. You may revoke Google permissions at any time via your Google account security settings.
  5. RefManager stores extension data in browser storage and, when enabled, Google Drive-related storage endpoints.
  6. Team/shared libraries should only be used with users who are authorized to view your references.

This agreement is intended for personal/scientific workflow use and does not replace legal/compliance advice.
